**ShareBill AI** is a smart, AI-powered receipt scanner and bill splitter. Simply snap a photo of your receipt, and let Gemini AI automatically extract items, prices, tax, and tip. Add your friends and assign items with a single tap to see exactly who owes what.

## ‚ú® Features

- **AI Extraction**: Uses Google Gemini 3.0 Pro to read complex receipts instantly.
- **Visual Enhancements**: AI-driven photo editing to clarify blurry or dark receipts.
- **Dynamic Splitting**: Assign items to multiple people; the app handles the math.
- **Proportional Fees**: Tax and tip are automatically distributed based on individual spend.
- **Privacy First**: All processing happens via secure API calls; no data is stored permanently.
